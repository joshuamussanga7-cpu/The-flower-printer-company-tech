Privacy Policy for Rain Escape

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

At The Flower Printers Company Tech, we believe in complete transparency and user privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile game, Rain Escape.

1. No Data Collection

Rain Escape is a fully offline game. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or device information from our players.

  • No Name or Contact Info
  • No Location Tracking
  • No Device ID tracking
  • No Analytics

2. Offline Gameplay

The game functions entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection to play. Any progress you make in the game is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Rain Escape does not use any third-party APIs, advertising networks, or social media integrations that could collect your data.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, Rain Escape is safe for players of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. However, our commitment to "No Data Collection" will remain the core of our philosophy for Rain Escape.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@flowerprinters.com.
